\section*{Syllabus}

\subsection*{Unit 2: Astronomical Techniques}
Telescopes: size and light gathering power; resolving power; different types of optical telescopes (reflecting and refracting); space telescopes; concept of virtual observatory; virtual observatory tools in astronomy: SIMBAD, Aladin; SDSS, AAVSO, Sky-View; introduction to photometry; CCD: an introduction; spectroscopy and polarimetry.
